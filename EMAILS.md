# PrintGet Email Addresses & Roles

All emails are on the `@printget.in` domain.

| Email | Full Address | Role / Purpose | Used In |
|---|---|---|---|
| orders | orders@printget.in | New order confirmations & order-related notifications sent to customers | Order confirmation emails, transactional notifications |
| support | support@printget.in | Primary shop partner & customer support inbox | Contact Page, Privacy Policy (data deletion requests), Terms Page |
| hello | hello@printget.in | Partner verification codes and welcome onboard emails (desktop signup) | Desktop partner auth — OTP + welcome (deliverability) |
| noreply | noreply@printget.in | System-generated emails (alerts, automated notifications) | Not used for partner signup (spam risk) |
| info | info@printget.in | General public-facing enquiries, onboarding new shop partners | About Page, general website footer |
| contact | contact@printget.in | Alternative public contact form submissions | Web app contact form |
| billing | billing@printget.in | Payment issues, invoice disputes, settlement queries from shop partners | Refund Policy, financial dispute communications |
| admin | admin@printget.in | Internal platform administration, shop approvals, account management | Internal use only (not published on legal pages) |
| team | team@printget.in | Internal team communications, partner onboarding coordination | Internal use only |

---

## Usage Guidelines

### Legal Pages
- **Contact Page** → `support@printget.in`
- **Privacy Policy** (data deletion requests) → `support@printget.in`
- **Refund Policy** (refund disputes) → `billing@printget.in`
- **Terms & Conditions** (general legal queries) → `info@printget.in`

### Web App
- **Footer** → `info@printget.in` (general) + `support@printget.in` (help)
- **Contact Form** → Routes to `contact@printget.in`
- **Order Emails** → Sent from `orders@printget.in`

### Desktop App
- **Partner Support** → `support@printget.in`
- **Billing/Settlement** → `billing@printget.in`
- **Partner signup (OTP + welcome)** → Sent from `hello@printget.in` only

---

> **Note:** `admin@printget.in` and `team@printget.in` are internal-only and should **not** be published on any public-facing page.
